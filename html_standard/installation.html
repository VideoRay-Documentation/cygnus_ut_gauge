Installation and Removal

Installation consists of installing the Cygnus Ultrasonic Thickness Gauge and mounting it onto the ROV. These procedures are described below:

  1. Attach the skid to the ROV using the standard screws and washers through the dome rings.

  2. Connect the cable to the Cygnus Ultrasonic Thickness Gauge at the rear of the Swing Arm Manipulator and to the ROV's accessory port. Make sure to seal the female end of the 9 pin connector with a terminating dummy plug.

Software installation is required before using the Thickness Gauge.
