The 3 Point Check

Perform the 3 Point check procedure to identify existing and potential problems. The most frequent reasons found to cause difficulty getting readings are:

  1. Is the Probe-membrane fitted correctly?

    Check that there is a thin layer of oil between the membrane and Probe-face, and with no air-bubbles trapped.

  2. Is the Probe-lead OK?

    Check to ensure that the probe lead is in good condition and is correctly attached to the gauge.

  3. Is the material measurable at all?

    Are the front and back faces of the material parallel? Is the material too heavily corroded? Is the material too thin for the Probe being used?

It is imperative that the user follows servicing guidelines when cleaning the Thickness Gauge.
