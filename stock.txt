<ol>
   <li>Unscrew the Knurled Ring from the end of the probe.<p></p></li>
   
   <li>Use the Locking Ring Key to unscrew the Locking Ring Ring from inside the Knurled Ring. The old membrane can be removed and discarded.<p></p></li>
   
   <li>Place a new membrane into the end of the Knurled Ring, ensuring it locates in the groove.<p></p></li>
   
   <li>Screw the Locking Ring back inside the Knurled Ring and tighten with the Locking Ring Key.<p></p></li>
   
   <li>Place a few drops of Membrane Couplant on to the probe face.<p></p></li>
   
   <li>Screw the Knurled Ring back onto the probe. Use your thumb to squeeze the couplant from under the membrane as you tighten the Knurled Ring down.<p></p></li>
   
   <li>You should see the membrane has a very thin film of couplant between itself and the probe face with no air bubbles.</li>
   </ol>
